Introduction to Foxboro I/A Series DCS Architecture Course
['Essential']
<p>The web-based course includes one content module and one assessment. The content module provides topics on specific tasks.&nbsp;<br>
The assessment is designed to evaluate the skills and knowledge that you gained by completing the content modules.</p>

<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong>&nbsp;<br>
- Newly hired Control Engineers</p>

<p><strong>Learning Objectives:</strong>&nbsp;<br>
After completing this course, you can:&nbsp;<br>
- Identify the benefits of a DCS.&nbsp;<br>
- Identify the hardware components of the Foxboro I/A Series DCS architecture.&nbsp;<br>
- Identify the functions of the hardware components of the Foxboro I/A Series DCS.&nbsp;<br>
- identify the steps to connect major hardware components of the Foxboro I/A Series DCS.&nbsp;</p>
