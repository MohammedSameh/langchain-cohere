Automating Industrial Processes with EcoStruxure Automation Expert
['Professional']
<p>EcoStruxure™ Automation Expert is an industrial automation software that helps improve engineering efficiency and productivity. With EcoStruxure Automation Expert, you can deploy an event-based, agile system and adapt to changing business requirements.</p>

<p>In compliance with the IEC 61499 standard, EcoStruxure Automation Expert enables optimization of industrial operations. You can modularize and repurpose solutions to suit project requirements.</p>

<p>This course demonstrates the features and configuration techniques in EcoStruxure Automation Expert version 22.0.</p>

<p><strong>Learning Objectives</strong><br>
After completing this course, you can:<br>
• Explain the features of EcoStruxure Automation Expert.<br>
• Install EcoStruxure Automation Expert and reference libraries.<br>
• Develop function blocks per requirements.<br>
• Configure graphics and HMIs for the developed solutions.<br>
• Manage and monitor the deployment of solutions.<br>
• Configure interlocks and permissives in solutions.<br>
• Integrate field devices with the developed solutions.&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>
<br>

<p><strong>Prerequisites</strong><br>
• Knowledge of hardware and software components in Distributed Control Systems (DCSs)<br>
• Familiarity with the IEC 61499 standard</p>
