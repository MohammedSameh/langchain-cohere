Getting Started With Virtualization
['Essential']
<p>This course presents the benefits of virtualization, the components of virtualized control systems, and the process of establishing virtualized control systems.</p>

<p><strong>Target Audience:&nbsp;</strong><br>
• Application Engineers<br>
• Field Service Engineers</p>

<p>For optimal viewing, launch the courses in a Microsoft Internet Explorer® browser</p>

<p><strong>Learning Objectives:</strong><br>
After completing this course, you can:&nbsp;<br>
• Explain virtualization and offered solutions to existing industrial challenges.<br>
• Describe the main components of virtualized control systems.<br>
• Identify the workflow of building and maintaining virtualized control systems. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Prerequisites:</strong><br>
Basic knowledge of FoxboroTM Distributed Control System (DCS) architecture&nbsp;</p>
