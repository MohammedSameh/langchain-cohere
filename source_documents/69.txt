8922A Tricon CX / TriStation 1131 Configuration and Implementation Overview (Virtual)
['Essential']
This course covers Triconex™ Tricon CX configuration and implementation. Using Microsoft® Windows® based TriStation™ 1131 Developer’s Workbench software, you perform basic navigation techniques. The instructor demonstrates how to configure, program, test, and download a TriStation 1131 project with digital and analog I/O to a Tricon CX system. You gain practical experience in system operation, including downloading new programs and modifications to a Tricon CX and proper procedures for enabling and disabling I/O points.<br><br><strong>Learning Objectives</strong><br><br>After completing this course, you can:<br>• Describe the basic theory of operation of Tricon CX architecture, including:<br>&nbsp; - Component identification<br>&nbsp; - Module replacement<br>• Develop a TriStation 1131 Developer’s Workbench project with Function Block Diagram (FBD) programming.<br>• Download procedures to the Tricon CX system.<br>• Perform online maintenance, including enabling and disabling points and download procedures.<br>• Perform system administration.<br><br><strong>Prerequisites<br></strong><br>• Working knowledge of Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) in a safety environment<br>• Familiarity with basic electronics and maintenance procedures
