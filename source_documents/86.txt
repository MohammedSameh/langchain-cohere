5223 Control Software FDM with HART
['Professional']
This course introduces the major components and concepts for using HART instrumentation with Control Software Field Device Manager (FDM) for HART. In this course, you configure and connect to HART instruments using FDM for HART.&nbsp;<br><br>The course is ideal for personnel who work with HART instruments.<br><br><strong>Learning Objectives<br></strong><br>After completing this course, you can:<br>• List the requirements of HART functionality and network configurations.<br>• Describe the procedures to populate FDM with HART instrument templates by instrument model type.<br>• Set up, install, and commission HART equipment on Foxboro™ DCS.<br>• Illustrate how to perform maintenance functions on HART instruments using FDM with HART.<br><br><strong>Prerequisites<br></strong><br>• Minimum of 1 year of experience in process control and instrumentation<br>• 5001 Control Software Configuration Essentials
