Cybersecurity for Industrial Safety Systems
['Professional']
<p>This course provides essential knowledge on the implementation of cybersecurity for industrial safety systems. Topics include security for Safety Instrumented Systems (SISs), physical security measures, Triconex™ Tofino firewall configuration, and project-level security. In addition, the course includes practice exercises on configuring the Tofino firewall and user access control settings in TriStation™ 1131 Developer’s Workbench software.&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Target Audience&nbsp;</strong><br>
- Application Engineers<br>
- Field Service Engineers</p>

<p><strong>Learning Objectives</strong><br>
After completing this course, you can:&nbsp;<br>
- Configure cybersecurity for industrial safety systems.<br>
- Manage user access controls in TriStation 1131 software projects.</p>

<p><strong>Prerequisites</strong><br>
- Knowledge of industrial safety systems<br>
- Concepts of cybersecurity</p>
