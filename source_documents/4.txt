8905D Trident / Tri-GP: Identify Components and Replace Modules Overview (Virtual)
['Essential']
This course provides an overview of the Trident™ and Tri-GP™ systems, with a primary focus on maintenance and troubleshooting. Course topics cover Triple Modular Redundancy (TMR) architecture and system configuration, including field wiring, power distribution, and module capabilities.&nbsp;<br><br>The course helps you gain practical experience and general field maintenance. Utilizing Triconex™ Enhanced Diagnostic Monitor, you troubleshoot the system, respond to alarms, replace modules, and clear faults.&nbsp;<br><br><strong>Learning Objectives<br></strong><br>After completing this course, you can:&nbsp;<br>• Describe TMR architecture and Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) operation in a Trident or Tri-GP system.<br>• Identify the components of a Trident or Tri-GP controller.<br>• Replace modules online in a Trident or Tri-GP system.<br>• Identify and respond to alarms in a Trident or Tri-GP system.<br>• Use Enhanced Diagnostic Monitor to:<br>&nbsp; - Connect to the Triconex controller.<br>&nbsp; - Monitor status and troubleshoot the system.<br>&nbsp; - Identify, report, and clear faults.<br>&nbsp; - Collect events.<br><br><strong>Prerequisites<br></strong><br>• Working knowledge of Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) in a safety environment<br>• Familiarity with basic electronics and maintenance procedures
