8921D Tricon CX Safety System: Identify Hardware and Replace Modules Overview (Virtual)
['Essential']
This course provides an overview of the Tricon CX™ system, with a primary focus on maintenance and troubleshooting. Course topics cover the basic principles of Triple Modular Redundancy (TMR) architecture and system configuration, including field wiring, power distribution, and module capabilities.&nbsp;<br><br>The course helps you gain practical experience and general field maintenance. Using Triconex™ Enhanced Diagnostic Monitor, you troubleshoot the system, respond to alarms, replace modules, and clear faults.&nbsp;<br><br><strong>Learning Objectives</strong><br><br>After completing this course, you can:&nbsp;<br>• Describe TMR architecture and Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) operation in the Tricon CX system.<br>• Identify the components of the Tricon CX system.<br>• Replace modules online in the Tricon CX system.<br>• Identify and respond to alarms in the Tricon CX system.<br>• Use Enhanced Diagnostic Monitor to:<br>&nbsp; - Connect to the Tricon CX system.<br>&nbsp; - Monitor status and troubleshoot the system.<br>&nbsp; - Identify, report, and clear faults.<br>&nbsp; - Collect events.<br><br><strong>Prerequisites<br></strong><br>• Working knowledge of Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) in a safety environment<br>• Familiarity with basic electronics and maintenance procedures
