8965 Triconex Trilogger Comprehensive (Virtual)
['Professional']
This course provides detailed instruction and practical exercises for TriLogger™ interfaces and utilities. After the course introduces an overview of Tricon™ system configuration and TriStation™ 1131 Developer’s Workbench software, you begin to use TriLogger features. This course focuses on effectively using TriLogger modes to collect, analyze, and identify the trending of real-time control application data.
