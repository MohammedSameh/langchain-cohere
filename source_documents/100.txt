6350 Fundamentals of Pneumatic Instrument Maintenance
['Essential']
This maintenance course helps you practice proper equipment installation and maintenance.<br><br>In this course, you master industry-standard operating procedures for pneumatic instruments. You explore the principles of operation behind pneumatic devices and reinforce your comprehension through extensive lab exercises.&nbsp;<br><br>The course is ideal for personnel who supervise, maintain, or repair pneumatic instruments.<br><br><strong>Learning Objectives<br></strong><br>After completing this course, you can:&nbsp;<br>• Repair pneumatic instruments by calibration or parts replacement.<br>• Align motion and force-balance controllers.<br>• Calibrate selected pneumatic instruments.<br>• Verify proper repair or alignment of an instrument.<br><br><strong>Prerequisites<br></strong><br>• 6321 Measurement Principles for Technicians or equivalent knowledge<br>• High school physics and mathematics
