Configuring Historian Client
['Professional']
<p>To improve the production and enhance the performance of a plant, it is important to analyze the process data. This analysis provides you details about plant operations and enables you to create detailed real-time data analysis reports. You can share the reports with the customer to optimize plant operations, improve plant performance, and reduce costs. To analyze plant data, you need an efficient database system that enables you to capture and store plant data and distribute the data for analysis. The Wonderware® Historian enables you to do all of that.&nbsp;</p>

<p>This course provides essential knowledge on configuring Wonderware Historian Client to view data.&nbsp;</p>

<p>For optimal viewing, launch the course in a Microsoft Internet Explorer® browser.</p>

<p><strong>Course Structure:</strong>&nbsp;</p>

<p>The web-based course includes one content modules and one assessments. The content module provide topics on specific tasks. After reviewing the content module, take the assessment. The module assessment is designed to evaluate the skills and knowledge you have gained by taking the content module.&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Course Description:&nbsp;</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li>Expected Duration: 1.9 hours&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Delivery Type: Web-based training&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Target Audience: Graduate Engineers, Control Engineers&nbsp;</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Learning Objectives&nbsp;</strong></p>

<p>After completing this course, you can:&nbsp;</p>

<ul>
	<li>Explain the uses of Historian Client.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Describe the uses of the different applications available in Historian Client.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Configure a trend in Historian to view the historized data.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Create a query in Historian Client to retrieve data from the Historian database.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Configure the Historian Client Workbook to view the historized data.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Configure the Historian Client Report to view the historized data.</li>
</ul>
