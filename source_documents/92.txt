Site Planning for Virtualized Control Systems
['Essential']
<p>This course describes the requirements that you need to consider prior to control system configuration. To help you establish virtualized control systems, this course introduces sizing guidelines of different Virtual Machines (VMs).</p>

<p><strong>Target Audience:&nbsp;</strong><br>
• Application Engineers<br>
• Field Service Engineers</p>

<p>For optimal viewing, launch the courses in a Microsoft Internet Explorer® browser</p>

<p><strong>Learning Objectives:</strong><br>
After completing this course, you can:&nbsp;<br>
• Gather system requirements for site planning.<br>
• Identify considerations and recommendations for filling each field of a site-planning document.<br>
• Fill in the site-planning document.</p>

<p><strong>Prerequisites:</strong><br>
Basic knowledge of FoxboroTM Distributed Control System (DCS) architecture&nbsp;</p>
