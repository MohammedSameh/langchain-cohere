Understanding the Basics of SCADA Systems for Process Control
['Essential']
<p>This course provides essential knowledge on Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) system functions. SCADA systems gather real-time information from remote locations for data processing, analysis, and equipment control. The course covers fundamental concepts on SCADA and the related hardware and software used for implementing a SCADA system.</p>
