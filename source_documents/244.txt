8921 Tricon CX Basic Maintenance (Virtual)
['Essential']
This basic-level maintenance course helps improve plant reliability and utilization by covering all key features of the Tricon CX™ system, with a focus on maintenance. In this course, you acquire practical experience using Triconex™ Enhanced Diagnostic Monitor to troubleshoot the system.&nbsp;<br><br>Lab exercises cover communications setup, maintenance on alarm identification and response, analysis of real-time diagnostic error messages, and the collection and review of system events. Written exams and performance evaluations during the course help validate your acquisition of expected learning objectives.<br><br>This course is ideal for personnel who maintain online safety-critical applications.<br><br><strong>Learning Objectives<br></strong><br>After completing this course, you can:&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>• Describe the basic theory of operation of Triple Modular Redundancy (TMR) architecture in a Tricon CX system, including modules, communications, and field terminations.<br>• Identify Tricon CX system components.<br>• Replace modules<br>• Recognize and respond to system, hardware, and field alarms.<br>• Monitor system status and troubleshoot alarms using Enhanced Diagnostic Monitor.<br><br><strong>Prerequisites<br></strong><br>• Working knowledge of Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) in a safety environment<br>• Familiarity with basic electronics and maintenance procedures
