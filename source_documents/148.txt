I/A Series System - Field Device Integration
['Professional']
<p>This course introduces the I/A Series System - Field Device Integration. Device integration is the process of connecting all devices in a plant, to the I/A Series® system, for efficient management and control. When devices are correctly integrated, you can control hardware in all supervisory, plant performance, and production management activities. Schneider Electric offers a variety of integration options to help you effectively track processes, maximize control, and ensure optimum output.</p>

<p>For optimal viewing, launch the courses in a Microsoft Internet Explorer® browser.</p>

<p>Duration: 3 hours</p>

<p>Delivery Type: Web-based training</p>

<p>Target Audience: Newly hired Control Engineers</p>

<p><strong>Learning Objectives:</strong></p>

<p>After completing this course, you can:</p>

<p>- Describe third-party device integration with Invensys Distributed Control Systems (DCSs).</p>

<p>- Explain Field Device System Integrators (FDSIs) in detail.</p>

<p>- Analyze the configuration of Fieldbus Modules (FBMs).</p>

<p>- Identify functional and environmental requirements of FDSIs.</p>

<p>- Describe redundancy in FDSI modules.</p>

<p>- Explain a Termination Assembly (TA) and its wiring</p>

<p><strong>Prerequisites:</strong><br>
• Six months of experience working with plant instrumentation and controls<br>
• Completion of courses in the Process Automation Basics Pack and Control Engineering Foundations Pack, or equivalent knowledge</p>
