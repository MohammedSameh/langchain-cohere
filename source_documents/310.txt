SAUTR1203-1 Citect SCADA: Configuring the Workspace and Adopting Situational Awareness (Virtual)
['Essential']
In this course, you gain insight into Citect™ Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) features and functionality for project design. You also become familiar with the new concept of Situational Awareness. The course includes interactive, hands-on practice using a starter project.<br><br><strong>Course Objectives</strong><br><br>After completing this course, you can:&nbsp;<br>• Design and develop projects based on Situational Awareness (SA).<br>• Utilize key features, such as SA Library, composite genies, auto-filing, faceplates, and interlocks, to help improve the safety and reliability of your plant operation.<br><br><strong>Specific Course Topics<br></strong><br>This course includes the following topics.<br><br>• Situational Awareness Projects<br>• Prepare an Equipment Hierarchy and Alarms<br>• Create Content Pages<br>• Enable Navigation<br>• Add Library Objects to Pages<br>• Configure and Manage Alarms<br>• Associate Faceplates with Equipment<br>• Add Content to a Level-2 page<br>• Use Interlocks<br><br><strong>Prerequisites<br></strong><br>• Basic understanding of Citect SCADA<br>• Familiarity with Microsoft® Windows operating systems<br>• Knowledge of Human Machine Interface (HMI) operating principles<br>• Experience in Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) control system design or programming (preferred)
