Using PSA Cloud for Dynamic Operational Risk Analyses
['Expert']
<p>EcoStruxure™ Process Safety Advisor (PSA) Cloud displays information about the performance and related operational risks of industrial plant assets. PSA Cloud includes functionality for running what-if scenarios and assessing the impact of changes in the status of Independent Protection Layers (IPLs).<br>
<br>
This course imparts essential knowledge on accessing PSA Cloud; creating roles and user groups, viewing users in PSA Cloud; filling Enterprise and Site Provision templates; and editing Enterprise and Site settings. Additionally, this course covers details about viewing and interpreting potential operational risks using the dynamic risk matrix.<br>
<br>
Detailed demonstrations, practices, and job aids help you effectively perform key tasks in PSA Cloud.</p>

<p><strong>Target Audience</strong>:<br>
• Application Engineers<br>
• Field Service Engineers</p>

<p><br>
<strong>Learning Objectives:</strong><br>
After completing this course, you can:<br>
• Explain the features and functions of PSA Cloud.<br>
• Manage users in PSA Cloud.<br>
• Fill in the Enterprise and Site Provision templates.<br>
• Edit Enterprise and Site settings through <strong>Admin Console</strong>.<br>
• View and interpret risks related to a potential hazardous event in PSA Cloud.<br>
• Investigate Safety Instrumented Function (SIF) demands in PSA Cloud.<br>
• Forecast risks related to potential hazardous events.<br>
<br>
