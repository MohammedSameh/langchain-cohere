Troubleshooting Foxboro DCS Using Scripts
['Professional']
<p>This course presents some of the common challenges you may face and offers how you can troubleshoot Foxboro DCS using scripts.</p>

<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Application Engineers, Maintenance Engineers, Plant Operations Manager, Customer/Field Service Engineers.</p>

<p>Launch this content in a Microsoft® Internet Explorer® or Mozilla® Firefox® browser. Do not launch the content in a Google® Chrome™ browser.</p>

<p><strong>Learning Objectives:</strong><br>
After completing this course, you can troubleshoot Foxboro DCS using the following scripts:<br>
• Database synchronization&nbsp;<br>
• Save all and load all<br>
• dbvu<br>
• ICC Print</p>

<p><strong>Prerequisites:</strong><br>
• Knowledge of Foxboro DCS architecture<br>
• Knowledge of System Manager<br>
• Knowledge of Ethernet Networks</p>
